The Greek parallel no less than ‘stulti,’ proves that Maternus’ remark
is an aphorism of general application and not, as Peter stranger con-

MS. reading, is, therefore, untenable. The only methodical emendation is
vocitant, the corruption being, as in innumerable similar cases, due to an
abbreviation which was subsequently misunderstood or not recognised.
